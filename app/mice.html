M.I.C.E - hinterland

×

We’re introducing you to Goa’s best kept secret – its hinterland. Situated on the west coast of India, Goa tells a story of its own. Its scenic landscapes of hills and mountains, its farmlands and lush green paddy fields account for it being a refreshing and rejuvenating destination.

We help conceptualise and execute corporate functions and events in the midst of Mother Nature’s most beautiful creations. Discover places which are tucked away in the hidden pockets of the Goan woods. Goa’s enclaves are also home to a variety of wildlife and bird sanctuaries that make it an ideal place, for those who want to witness rare species of birds and animals in their natural habitat.

Break free from being surrounded by the monotonous claddings of the office walls and let nature be the host to your corporate event. From gatherings on a Yacht on Chorao River, to a conference amidst the serenity of Dudhsagar falls or a team building activity at a farm, Goa’s hinterland will amaze you in every way.

M.I.C.E - heritage

×

The true charm of Goa lies within its cultural heritage. The Architecture in Goa is one of the remaining accounts of the wide spectrum of its rich heritage. The Architecture retains the functional elements of the pre-colonial period but also showcases the subsequent influence of European architecture with its elements of classical, gothic and manueline design forms. A combination of Indian and Portuguese architecture in the form of churches, temples, cathedrals, heritage houses and it grand forts, these places have a special cultural and physical significance. Moreover, the churches and convents in Old Goa are a UNESCO Wold heritage site.

With the legends and stories associated to all these places, a MICE event at these places is simply one of a kind. We have conceptualized various corporate events that can be executed in the midst of these historic monuments.

Alternately many of the resorts and hotels in Goa have drawn inspiration from this heritage which is evidently seen in their design and architecture. A setup at such locations will also rightly soak in the elements of heritage and bring a different dimension to the whole event.

Take advantage of this opportunity to reminiscence and celebrate your event at any of these glorious structures.
