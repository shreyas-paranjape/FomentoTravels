Activities

×

When it comes to activities in Goa you are spoilt for choice. We offer the perfect bunch of activities to suit every varied interest of the traveller. Our activity experts have on boarded the best vendors after thorough quality and safety checks.

Goa is one of the best beach destinations in India making it an ideal place for water sports and under water activities. Get your adrenaline rush through water sports activities like jet skiing, scuba diving, sailing, parasailing, diving, kayaking etc.

The thickly forested mountains and abundance of hilly areas contribute to it being a perfect place for adventure sport enthusiast as well as nature lovers. For them some of the activities which we provide are rock climbing, trekking, monsoon rides and leisure forest and nature camps. The ones amused by Goa’s architectural heritage can join the heritage walks.

Goa’s versatile range of activity avenues will rejuvenate you and sometimes also test your physical strength, together contributing to you experiencing the true sense of coastal and tropical living.
