Beach Resorts

CIDADE DE GOA

Cidade De Goa is a luxurious beach resort spread across an area of 40 acres of lush green landscape. Walk through the resort to the beautiful beach of Vainguinim valley and enjoy the best of the both worlds as you sip on cocktails at the poolside bar while enjoying scintillating sunset views. This resort is one of the most iconic resorts in Goa with its intriguing Portuguese and Iberian architecture.


BAYWATCH RESORT

Baywatch Resort is nestled on the serene coastline of Sernabatim Beach in Colva, this contemporary and luxurious resorts offers elegant and spacious rooms and suites equipped with well-appointed amenities to make you have a memorable holiday in Goa.


RAMADA CARAVELA BEACH RESORT

Located on the serene sands of Varca Beach, Ramada Caravela Beach Resort is a lush property, aesthetically designed to give you a rejuvenating experience. Each of the rooms and villas has a private balcony that overlooks either the ocean, landscaped gardens or one of Goa's largest pool. A 9 hole golf course, a Casino and an Ayurveda Centre, differentiates this from any other five star in Goa.


ZURI WHITE SANDS

The Zuri White Sands in Goa sits on a picturesque white-sand beach, Varca. The resort boasts of a spectacular view of the sea from their Goan styled luxury rooms. The restaurant offers authentic Goan, European and Pan Asian cuisine that is sure to tickle your taste buds.


HOLIDAY INN RESORT

Holiday Inn Resort is located at Mobor beach, Cavelossim, Goa. This five star beach resort has a huge property with a captivating style that combines traditional Goan architecture with contemporary design and luxurious finishing. You can choose spacious accommodation in a stunning beachfront setting with the option of a garden view, pool view or sea view.

Night Life

The Big Band

From jazz and blues to sing along tracks from the 60’s, from bollywood tunes to karaoke nights – Goa’s pubs and restaurants are host to it all. You can start your night early with a meal at any of our recommended locations and groove to the foot tapping music. Cavala and Cohiba are two such places up north that showcase local bands playing rock and retro. The place is perfect for those who like to dance to live music and engage in friendly chatter.


Raves and Grooves

Goa truly lives up to its name as the party capital of India. If you simply want to let your hair down, drink, dance and have the carefree Goan experience head to the Tito’s lane where you will find a number of inviting clubs. Sinq in Sinquerim is a more sophisticated club which hosts a number of international DJ’s. Tito’s at Radisson Blu offers unique themed nights like ‘Divas Invite’ – Ladies Nights, ‘Bollywood Madness Nights’ etc. Hill top in Anjuna is an ideal party place for trance lovers. The music picks up by sunset and has parties that last until the last raver is standing.


The Sea and the Gambler

Celebrate, eat, dance and get lucky at any of the magnificent floating casinos in Goa. Goa has the highest number of off shore and on shore Casinos in the country making it your favourite get away for gaming and entertainment. The Deltin floating Casinos are luxurious and are a delight to non-gamblers as well, owing to their gourmet restaurants, kids room and live entertainment.


Night Bazaar

The night markets in Goa give you a chance to unwind while you eat, drink and shop under the moonlight. The centre stage at the ‘Saturday Night Market’ in Arpora has live performances by talented artists and bands from across the globe. The markets open the minute the sun sets below the mesmerizing skyline. Be prepared to explore an array of whimsical items and flexible price tags, browsing everything from food items to clothes to authentic antiques.

Bike Riding

Ride along the roads less travelled, hire a bike or a bicycle and feel an immediate connection to your surroundings. Discover hidden beauties with a day trip to the mesmerizing Island of Diva or a ride through the vibrant villages in South Goa. If you want to be acquainted with the city life, ride through the Cities of Panjim or Maragao and be a part of the daily ebbs and flows of Goa’s unique lifestyle.


Spas and Yoga Retreats

The Spa’s in Goa, gives you the chance to rejuvenate and renew your mind, body and spirit in a sophisticated style with no effort from your end. All you need to do is relax and take in the sensational aromas while you are being pampered. If you are willing to make an effort to harmonize your mind, body and soul, sign up for beach yoga classes held by trained professionals.


Spice Farms

A trip to any of the spice farms in Goa is rejuvenating to all the senses. These farms are mostly located in villages that are serene and untouched and strive to show you the perks of rural living in lush surroundings. A few things to look forward to at these farms are - elephant washing and riding, cultural dances, and guided walks through the plantations and the very scrumptious cuisine.


Shopping

Just like everything else in Goa, the shopping too is an experience and not just an activity. The weekly flea markets are a colourful affair with shoppers buzzing, bargaining, eating and some tapping their feet to beats of the drums and other instruments that are also up for sale! For those who want to do some serious shopping, MG Road in Panjim is where you will find an assortment of local and branded stores.


Heritage/Photo Walks

Rich heritage, lush green views, mesmerizing sunsets, ever-smiling locals and vibrant architecture – Goa is ideal for photography enthusiasts to indulge in heritage and photo walks. Capture the rich heritage of Goa through guided walks in Old Goa or choose to have your solo adventure with a walk down the winding alleys of Fontainhas.


Fishing

Including a fishing trip in your itinerary is sure to bring about unexpected enthusiasm and fishing in Goa will truly be a rewarding adventure! The locals are fishing experts and will be more than willing to help you learn the tricks of the trade. Enjoy fishing trips by the beach or at a serene river side location.


Dining

Goa has an overwhelming choice of renowned restaurants. Some are known for their scrumptious Goan cuisine, some for romantic atmosphere and some for modern explorative cuisine.  Goa boasts of a number of restaurants that give you unforgettable dining experiences. If your stay is short you will need to make difficult decisions like choosing between Thalassa in Vagator for fresh Greek food combined with breath taking sunset views or Mums Kitchen in Panjim for authentic Goan cuisine and a sussegad Goan experience. Hit us up for details on restaurants, it’s our favourite area of expertise.

Venues

KALA ACADEMY

An Iconic Charles Correa Design, Kala Academy as the name suggests, has been a host to the celebration of art and artists. Located right on the banks of river Mandovi, It has a well-equipped Auditorium along with an open amphitheatre. This venue has witnessed many conferences and events. Amongst worth mentioning is the International Film Festival of India.


SHYAM PRASAD MUKHERJEE STADIUM

Located on top the Taleigao Plateau, this newly built indoor stadium is fully air conditioned can be a good venue for any big scaled event. It’s one of the largest indoor halls in the state and also has hosted the Lusofonia Games.


ESG COURTYARD

Housing the iconic Escola Médico-Cirúrgica de Nova Goa building, this is the venue where the International Film Festival of India (IFFI) is held every year. It also plays host to many other popular festivals of Goa such as the Wine Festival and the Sea Food Festival.


PENHA DE FRANCA

Located in Brittona, facing the Mandovi River, the venue can hold a maximum of eight hundred people. It has a spacious dance floor and lawn area. Normally used for weddings and parties, it can also be utilised for post conference events.


EMERALD LAWNS

One of the biggest outdoor lawns in Goa and one of the most popular too, it has an amazing set up with an indoor hall and an outdoor stage area. It can be used to organise a large party or gala event.

Water Sports

Parasailing

In this thrilling activity a person is attached to a parachute and is towed behind a speed boat. The boat circles the blue waters while you soar sky high and enjoy glorious coastal views. You will be equipped with safety gear and will be briefed before you take off the white sands.


Scuba-Diving

Witness the aquatic life with this underwater activity. Equipped with a wet suit and a breathing apparatus, Scuba-Diving makes it possible for you to grow gills and explore a whole new world, a new eco-system and much more as you scuba-dive into the exotic underwater spots by the beautiful sea.


Banana Boat

This fun and exciting water activity comprises of a yellow banana shaped tube which holds 6-8 people and is pulled by a speed-jetty while the passengers try to hold on and prevent a capsize which is the inevitable result and a splash of fun.


Jet Ski

If boats are the cars of the waters, the Jet Ski’s are the two-wheeler of the seas. Offered in most of the beaches of the State you will surely enjoy speeding through the waters with these mean machines.


Snorkelling

While Scuba diving will need you to immerse yourself completely into a certain depth of a water body, another simpler option to observe the sea or aquatic life can be through snorkelling. Equipped with a diving mask, a snorkel, fins and in some cases wet suits, this is an exhilarating experience of exploring or having a glimpse into another part of life which is normally out of sight for us.


Kayaking

Take part in this adventurous and unforgettable experience and explore the breathtaking beauty of Goa’s blue waters with your own Canoe or by Kayaking. Explore the rivers of Mandovi and Zuari and the backwaters of Sal which are ideal for this scenic and thrilling activity.


Sailing

Invest time learning the art of Sailing or ask to be accompanied by one of the friendly guides, the discretion is yours! Sailing holds endless possibilities for all, the thrill of steering your own boat and greeting friendly dolphins along the way is absolutely fulfilling.

adventure

Paragliding

Glide gently through the air while you soak in views of Goa’s dramatic coastline and the deep blue Indian Ocean. Whatever your experience level, you will be given all the necessary instructions and a comprehensive safety briefing for a smooth and safe flight.


Paintballing

Get into your best camouflages because there is war with colours coming your way. Paintball is a game where the participants play, either in a team or individually and try to knock out the opponents by shooting them with paintballs loaded in a gun. The game can either be played to capture a flag or paint down all the members of the opposing team.


White Water Rafting

White water rafting or simply rafting is said to be an extreme sport. Paddling your way through various degrees of rough waters, you will be dealing with risk and an adrenalin-filled experience where teamwork will be required for you to escape fatalities. Enjoy these experiences in various locations across the State.


Treks & Hikes

There are several spots in Goa which allow you to tread over steeper grounds or more testing conditions while enjoying the greenery around you. The Arambol Sweet Water Trek or Dudhsagar falls are just two of many locations in Goa where you will find challnges. So explore in higher terrains and test yourself in the many opportunities of Treks & Hikes across Goa.


Go-karting

Experience the F1 life without the effort of daily practice and innumerable attempts to get funding.-karting also known as Kart Racing is a form of open-wheel motorsport where one can drive with little or no inhibitions.


ATV Rides

Want to be the king of all roads, well just rent out an ATV and explore the State that offers you just what you are looking for. All-terrain Vehicles are four-wheel drive vehicles, which give you the freedom to ride in all possible land conditions without slowing you down.


Rock-Climbing

The true test of strength is not of physical kind but a spiritual one. Which is probably true, but who wouldn’t want to climb up a dangerously steep incline of a high rock, which would probably give you an adrenalin rush of a lifetime. While being attached to a harness, which makes sure you don’t plummet into a horrible crash, this is the ultimate adventure sports and tests all phobias in one activity.
