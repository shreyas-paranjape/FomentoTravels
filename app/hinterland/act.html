Activities

Paintball

Get into your best camouflages because there is war with colours coming your way. Paintball is a game where the participants play, either in a team or individually and try to knock out the opponents by shooting them with paintballs loaded in a gun. The game can either be played to capture a flag or paint down all the members of the opposing team. A great way to build coherent and focussed team as well as instil leadership qualities.


White Water Rafting

Paddling your way through various degrees of rough waters, you will be dealing with risk and an adrenalin-filled experience where teamwork will be required for you to escape fatalities. Build a team which knows how to work under extreme conditions with limited resources at their disposal through this activity.


Treasure Hunts

Put your team’s analytical and logical thinking to test with our fun filled and thrilling treasure hunts. Teams get a specific timeframe to decode the puzzles and clues to make it to the finish line. These treasure hunts can also be designed within resorts or theme parks or spice farms.


Rock Climbing

The true test of strength is not of physical kind but a spiritual one. Which is probably true, but who wouldn’t want to climb up a dangerously steep incline of a high rock, which would probably give you an adrenalin rush of a lifetime. This is the ultimate adventure sports, tests all phobias in one activity and identifies your risk appetite.
