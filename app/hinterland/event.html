Event ideas

Employee outbound training at a spice farm

Spice farms provide a holistic environment for outbound corporate training and off-sites. So, shed your inhibitions, break barriers and learn the intricacies of farm life while you train at any one of the rural spice farms.


Incentive travel to an Eco resort

Moving away from the beaches and shacks, hinterlands offer a wide variety of options which are yet unexplored. Eco resorts located in pristine locations get you in touch with nature giving you the real taste of Goa. Combining luxury with nature, this is one richly rewarding experience your star performers or valued sales partners would need.


Management/Leadership development programs through treks and nature trails

An adventurous way to learn the skills required to be a leader is trekking, this sport gives you many lessons to take back to your personal and professional life. The expedition is designed in such a way that it gives employees the confidence and discretion to make their own decisions, work in a team and achieve a common goal along with yours peers


Team building exercises through organic farming

Give something back to the environment while you get your hands dirty while you experience Organic Farming on lush green fields. We set out parameters for what the team has to achieve while we watch as the team draws a plan, gets messy and makes the most out of available resources.
