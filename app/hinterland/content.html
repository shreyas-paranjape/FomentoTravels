We’re introducing you to Goa’s best kept secret – its hinterland. Situated on the west coast of India, Goa tells a story of its own. Its scenic landscapes of hills and mountains, its farmlands and lush green paddy fields account for it being a refreshing and rejuvenating destination.

We help conceptualise and execute corporate functions and events in the midst of Mother Nature’s most beautiful creations. Discover places which are tucked away in the hidden pockets of the Goan woods. Goa’s enclaves are also home to a variety of wildlife and bird sanctuaries that make it an ideal place, for those who want to witness rare species of birds and animals in their natural habitat.

Break free from being surrounded by the monotonous claddings of the office walls and let nature be the host to your corporate event. From gatherings on a Yacht on Chorao River, to a conference amidst the serenity of Dudhsagar falls or a team building activity at a farm, Goa’s hinterland will amaze you in every way.
