Water Sports

Parasailing

In this thrilling activity a person is attached to a parachute and is towed behind a speed boat. The boat circles the blue waters while you soar sky high and enjoy glorious coastal views. You will be equipped with safety gear and will be briefed before you take off the white sands.


Scuba-Diving

Witness the aquatic life with this underwater activity. Equipped with a wet suit and a breathing apparatus, Scuba-Diving makes it possible for you to grow gills and explore a whole new world, a new eco-system and much more as you scuba-dive into the exotic underwater spots by the beautiful sea.


Banana Boat

This fun and exciting water activity comprises of a yellow banana shaped tube which holds 6-8 people and is pulled by a speed-jetty while the passengers try to hold on and prevent a capsize which is the inevitable result and a splash of fun.


Jet Ski

If boats are the cars of the waters, the Jet Ski’s are the two-wheeler of the seas. Offered in most of the beaches of the State you will surely enjoy speeding through the waters with these mean machines.


Snorkelling

While Scuba diving will need you to immerse yourself completely into a certain depth of a water body, another simpler option to observe the sea or aquatic life can be through snorkelling. Equipped with a diving mask, a snorkel, fins and in some cases wet suits, this is an exhilarating experience of exploring or having a glimpse into another part of life which is normally out of sight for us.


Kayaking

Take part in this adventurous and unforgettable experience and explore the breathtaking beauty of Goa’s blue waters with your own Canoe or by Kayaking. Explore the rivers of Mandovi and Zuari and the backwaters of Sal which are ideal for this scenic and thrilling activity.


Sailing

Invest time learning the art of Sailing or ask to be accompanied by one of the friendly guides, the discretion is yours! Sailing holds endless possibilities for all, the thrill of steering your own boat and greeting friendly dolphins along the way is absolutely fulfilling.
