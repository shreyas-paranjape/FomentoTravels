team building

Beach Football

Everybody loves to kick the football. Experience the joy of playing football on the soft white sands of Goa. Test your strategies and team synergies as you hit the beach to have some competitive fun. Have a goal post of only 1 metre to keep things interesting.


Treasure Hunts

We organize fun and thrilling treasure hunts. Teams are given a particular time frame and clues to solve. Treasure hunt can take place in the city by foot or car, treasure hunts can also be designed within resorts or theme parks.


Water Sports and Sailing

Sailing provides a platform to help teams improve on specific areas and skills. We conduct tailor- made programmes according to your business objectives. Successful sailing demands co-operation and good communication, both essential tools for smooth organisational functioning.


Paintballing

Paintball is a game where the participants play, either in a team or individually and try to knock out the opponents by shooting them with paintballs loaded in a gun. The game can either be played to capture a flag or paint down all the members of the opposing team.
