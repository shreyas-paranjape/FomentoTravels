adventure

Paragliding

Glide gently through the air while you soak in views of Goa’s dramatic coastline and the deep blue Indian Ocean. Whatever your experience level, you will be given all the necessary instructions and a comprehensive safety briefing for a smooth and safe flight.


White Water Rafting

White water rafting or simply rafting is said to be an extreme sport. Paddling your way through various degrees of rough waters, you will be dealing with risk and an adrenalin-filled experience where teamwork will be required for you to escape fatalities. Enjoy these experiences in various locations across the State.


Treks & Hikes

There are several spots in Goa which allow you to tread over steeper grounds or more testing conditions while enjoying the greenery around you. The Arambol Sweet Water Trek or Dudhsagar falls are just two of many locations in Goa where you will find challnges. So explore in higher terrains and test yourself in the many opportunities of Treks & Hikes across Goa.


Go-karting

Experience the F1 life without the effort of daily practice and innumerable attempts to get funding.-karting also known as Kart Racing is a form of open-wheel motorsport where one can drive with little or no inhibitions.


ATV Rides

Want to be the king of all roads, well just rent out an ATV and explore the State that offers you just what you are looking for. All-terrain Vehicles are four-wheel drive vehicles, which give you the freedom to ride in all possible land conditions without slowing you down.


Rock-Climbing

The true test of strength is not of physical kind but a spiritual one. Which is probably true, but who wouldn’t want to climb up a dangerously steep incline of a high rock, which would probably give you an adrenalin rush of a lifetime. While being attached to a harness, which makes sure you don’t plummet into a horrible crash, this is the ultimate adventure sports and tests all phobias in one activity.
