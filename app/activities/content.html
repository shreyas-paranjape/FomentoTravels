For them some of the activities which we provide are rock climbing, trekking, monsoon rides and leisure forest and nature camps. The ones amused by Goa’s architectural heritage can join the heritage walks.

Goa’s versatile range of activity avenues will rejuvenate you and sometimes also test your physical strength, together contributing to you experiencing the true sense of coastal and tropical living.
