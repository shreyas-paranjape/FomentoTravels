Event ideas

Conclaves at an Island

Are you planning a discreet conclave and want a venue that promises true discretion? Then the islands of Goa with rich history and cultural significance are a place to be. The unique locale of the exotic and famous islands like the Bat Island or the remote islands of Anjadip makes for a pleasant time spent in the paradise that is Goa.


Product Launch at a Fort

How about launching your product on a fort? Exciting, isn’t it? Forts like Reis Magos and Aguada which offer breath-taking views of the landscape and the coastline are conveniently located from the beaches and resorts. Such an exclusive setting will allow you to announce your new product to the world with the required grandeur and pomp.


Conference at a heritage resort

Arrange your next conference in a quaint and beautifully restored heritage resort. A perfect setting for an off-beat conference, for all your business partners and clients. Goa also features a array of resorts designed with influences from the Indo-Portuguese architecture.


Off-sites in a Portuguese mansion

Give a taste of the vintage Indo-Portuguese lifestyle to your employees with an off-site meeting in some of the massive Latin mansions in Goa. Let them enjoy the “Sushegad Lifestyle” here, a much needed break from the normal hustle and bustle of the city, while you discuss about your future business goals.
